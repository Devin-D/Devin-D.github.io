About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Oregon (go Ducks) studying Bioinformatics and Genomics. I enjoy the challenges of programming novel approaches to analyze data generated from next generation sequencing technologies. I am also a classically trained biologist, so I am not afraid to get behind the bench or out in the field and get my hands dirty.
In my spare time, if I am not outside exploring with my dog, you can find me reading comic books or watching a good movie.
